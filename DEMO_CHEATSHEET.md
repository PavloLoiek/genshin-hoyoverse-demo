## Talking Points Per Use Case

### 1. Account_Recovery
- **Business problem:** real-world account lockout is the #1 support volume driver for live-service games. Flat UID-required policy fails the 12-18% of callers who don't have it.
- **Stage-by-stage:**
  - S1: Policy knowledge only. Can answer "what do I do if I'm locked out" but can't actually recover.
  - S2: Can call `initiate_recovery` but skips verification — anyone can reset Kaito's password.
  - S3: Verifies via OTP, correct SOP — but stuck on Viktor-style edge cases (no UID, no email).
  - S4: Knowledge-based fallback (last 5-star + device + AR + region), platform-based fallback (PSN/Apple), empathy throughout.
- **Edge case:** Viktor Ivanov (UID 708817234), no UID + no email access.
- **Metrics to cite:** Recovery time from 24h (stage 3 escalation to human) → 4 min (stage 4 self-service-via-agent).
- **Tie-back:** "This exact pattern applies to every gaming platform. Anyone with a large free-to-play base has Viktors calling every hour."

### 2. Wish_And_Gacha_Disputes
- **Business problem:** gacha frustration is a top churn driver. Most frustrated players never call — they just quit.
- **Stage-by-stage:**
  - S1: Can explain pity system in the abstract.
  - S2: Can pull wish history and pity counter — but leaks data (no verification), shares spend info unsolicited.
  - S3: Reads the Pity Policy correctly — but robotically. Turns frustrated callers into churners.
  - S4: Empathises, explains *soft pity mechanics* (dramatically boosted 75–89), reassures, enables in-app tips.
- **Edge case:** Sophie Martin (UID 703321876), 82 pulls no 5-star on Furina, threatening to quit.
- **Metrics to cite:** At 82 pulls, soft pity has been active for 7 pulls — the character is statistically 1-10 wishes away. A human agent saves the player. A policy-quoting agent loses them.
- **Tie-back:** "How many subscribers does your best-retained customer touch each month? How many of those interactions are the difference between retention and churn?"

### 3. Top_Up_And_Refund
- **Business problem:** minor-account refund disputes end up in chargebacks, App Store complaints, and regulatory scrutiny. Mishandled refunds are expensive on both sides.
- **Stage-by-stage:**
  - S1: Can quote refund policy.
  - S2: Can process refunds — but does not check for `is_minor` flag or require guardian verification.
  - S3: Applies "all sales final" consistently — but denies legitimate Minor Account Protection refunds.
  - S4: Detects minor account, walks guardian verification, opens expedited ticket, offers parental controls setup.
- **Edge case:** Helen Williams (guardian), Emma Williams (UID 705532108, minor, AR 45), 520 USD across 3 days.
- **Metrics to cite:** A 520 USD chargeback costs ~$40 in fees + reputation; a Minor Account refund via proper verification costs 0.
- **Tie-back:** "Every platform regulator in the world is watching minor-account spending right now. The difference between stage 3 and stage 4 here is regulatory risk."

### 4. Compensation_And_Bugs
- **Business problem:** standardised compensation is fair but feels mechanical for high-value players. Goodwill is where loyalty is built.
- **Stage-by-stage:**
  - S1: Knows the published compensation table.
  - S2: Can issue compensation — but without confirming incident match or UID region.
  - S3: Applies the table correctly. But never escalates. Kaito lost his Season Pass milestone and got 300 Primogems flat.
  - S4: Applies the table + escalates goodwill (+3 Fates, +60 Primos, apology mail, grace period).
- **Edge case:** Kaito Nakamura (UID 832458917, Asia), affected by INC202604161 (18-hour Apr 16-17 outage). Season Pass milestone affected.
- **Metrics to cite:** Goodwill escalation adds ~$8 of virtual value. Churn reduction from one well-handled high-value complaint: $100+ lifetime.

## Troubleshooting

- **Deploy fails** — check the Render API key in `deploy/deploy.sh` is still valid; check service name availability on https://dashboard.render.com
- **Website not loading** — DNS propagation; wait 30 seconds and retry
- **Upload script fails** — verify Copilot base URL and auth token
- **Bot not finding UIDs** — IDs are case-insensitive; confirm no dashes/spaces in the input. UIDs are 9 digits, no delimiters.
- **Stage 4 edge case replay doesn't resolve** — confirm Stage 3 transcripts AND Stage 4 transcripts both reference the same UID/player name.
